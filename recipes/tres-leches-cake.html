Ingredients

For the Cake

  • 1 cup Bob’s Red Mill Unbleached White All Purpose Flour 136 grams.
  • 1½ teaspoons Bob’s Red Mill Baking Powder 6 grams.
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt.
  • 5 large eggs 250 grams, separated.
  • 1 cup granulated sugar 200 grams, divided.
  • ⅓ cup whole milk 76 grams.
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract 4 grams.
  • ½ teaspoon almond extract 2 grams.

For the Milk

  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk 396 grams (1 can).
  • 12 ounces evaporated milk 340 grams (1 can).
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream 114 grams.

For the Topping

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream 227 grams.
  • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract 2 grams.
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar 14 grams (use more if desired).
  • Ground cinnamon for dusting.
